Short Bio

I am currently a first year PhD student in the Computer Science Dept. of Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. Prof. Boleslaw K. Szymanski is my academic advisor in the first year. My recent research interests lie in algorithmic methods of data mining with statistical models, graphical models and learning algorithms, and their applications in complex networks (e.g., social networks), business intelligence and recommender systems.

Before that, I got my bachelor's degree in Communication Engineering from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in Chengdu, China.

In my spare time, I like programming :) As far as I can see, programming is a kind of art as same as writing (I like writing, too). Besides, I like reading, music, movie and photography. All of them make me discover and enjoy the beauty. I also like to play some sports. Soccer, badminton, tennis and pingpong are all fine to me.
